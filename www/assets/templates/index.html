What is Almost There?

Almost There is a new platform that makes creating a fundraising campaign as simple as it gets. You just need a good idea, we will take care of the rest!

Do you need a last push to achieve your goal? Publish your new videogame, get that last piece of equipment you need to become a film maker or buy the last version of that great software for designers.

Maybe you're going through some things? Your bicycle broke, you want to get back home for the holidays, or your cat needs a special diet and the food is so expensive! You'll get by with a little help from your friends.

You can do it, you're Almost There!

How does it work?

Almost There is possible thanks to NEAR. NEAR is a novel blockchain technology, that allows to decentralize applications. This means that your campaing, and the payments of your supporters, will be hosted and processed by a secure decentralized network. No middleman, no complications, just you, and your supporters. If your campaign gets funded, the network will automatically send you the money. If it doesnt, then the network will take care of refunding all your supporters. No middleman also means, lower fees! Your supporters will only pay a minimum amount (called gas) to keep the network running, the rest, is all for you!
